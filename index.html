CS221P Network Security: MEng in Cybersecurity, Ministry of Interior (MoI), Saudi Arabia.


I am an Assistant Professor in Computer Science at the Computer, Electrical and Mathematical Sciences and Engineering (CEMSE) Division, King Abdullah University of Science and Technology (KAUST), Saudi Arabia. I am also affiliated with the KAUST Center of Excellence for Generative AI (GenAI). Before that, I obtained my PhD from the City University of Hong Kong, my Master's degree from the Australian National University (ANU), and my Bachelor's degree from Shanghai Jiao Tong University (SJTU).

My research lies in the cross-discipline of cybersecurity and AI, including (1) AI safety and AI for security (e.g., adversarial attacks and defenses, LLM/VLM security, agentic AI security), (2) System security (e.g., IoT, eXtended Reality (XR), and robotics systems), and (3) Low-power mobile computing.

Hiring. I am actively looking for self-motivated Ph.D./Master students, postdocs and research interns. If you're passionate about these areas, please feel free to reach out through email and I will give you feedback as soon as possible. I highly suggest you to read this to know the admission process before sending me the email.
