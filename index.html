Tao Ni (Tony), Ph.D.


About Me. I am an Assistant Professor of Computer Science at the Computer, Electrical and Mathematical Sciences and Engineering (CEMSE) Division, King Abdullah University of Science and Technology (KAUST). I earned my Ph.D. from the City University of Hong Kong, my Master's degree from the Australian National University (ANU), and my Bachelor's degree from Shanghai Jiao Tong University (SJTU).

Research Interests. My research lies in the cross-discipline of cybersecurity, IoT, and AI, with a primary focus on Building Secure, Reliable and Efficient Cyber-Physical Systems (CPS) to achieve trustworthy computing and confidential sensing in critical cyber-infrastructures. My current research interests include: (1) CPS security (e.g., AIoT, eXtended Reality (XR), and autonomous driving), (2) Machine learning systems (MLSys) security (e.g., backdoor/adversarial attacks, on-device models), and (3) Battery-free CPS-IoT in mobile computing.

Hiring.I am actively looking for self-motivated Ph.D./Master students (starting from Fall 2026) and research interns interested in working on cybersecurity. If you're passionate about these areas, please feel free to reach out through email and I will try to give you feedback as soon as possible.
